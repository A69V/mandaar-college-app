Football

About the Game

Welcome to the Umang Mandar Football tournament! Football, the world's most popular sport, is a game of passion, strategy, and teamwork. Here at GEC Banka, it's where departments clash for glory on the field. Expect thrilling matches, incredible goals, and a roaring crowd.

Event Rules

  • Each team must consist of 11 players on the field and 5 substitutes.
  • All matches will be 60 minutes long (two 30-minute halves) with a 10-minute halftime break.
  • Standard FIFA rules apply unless otherwise specified by the coordinators.
  • A red card will result in the player's suspension for the next match. Two yellow cards in one match equal a red card.
  • All players must wear appropriate footwear (studs or trainers).
  • The referee's decision is final and must be respected by all teams.
