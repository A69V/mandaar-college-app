Welcome to
Umang Mandar

We are thrilled to welcome you to Umang Mandaar, the most anticipated and celebrated annual sports festival of Government Engineering College (GEC), Banka! This isn't just a competition; it's a vibrant celebration of our campus spirit. Umang Mandaar is born from a passion for sports, a belief in healthy competition, and a desire to bring our entire college community together. Organized by the GEC Banka Sports Club, our festival is a platform for students to showcase their athletic talents, forge new friendships, and learn the invaluable lessons of teamwork and sportsmanship. Whether you're an athlete ready to give your all, a friend cheering from the sidelines, or a newcomer eager to see what GEC Banka is all about, you are a vital part of this event. We invite you to join us, feel the electric atmosphere, and share in the joy and passion that make Umang Mandaar an unforgettable experience. Let the games begin!

Opening Ceremony

Football Match: Finals

Volleyball & Badminton

Athletics Meet

Indoor Championships

Prize Distribution & Closing

Honoring the winners and participants, followed by the official closing ceremony.
